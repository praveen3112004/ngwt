WHO WE ARE

Bridging the Gap Between Education & Industry

Founded with the vision to bridge the gap between traditional education and evolving industry needs, NEWGEN WINGS TECHNOLOGY has been a trusted name in IT training for over 9 years in Avadi, Chennai.

We offer cutting-edge curriculum, industry-expert mentors, and a dedicated placement team to ensure every student walks away with the skills and confidence to succeed in today's tech landscape.

Our commitment is simple — every student who walks through our doors leaves with job-ready skills, real-world project experience, and access to a powerful corporate network.

  • Industry-certified curriculum updated every semester
  • Expert mentors with 5–15 years of real-world experience
  • 50+ corporate hiring partners across Tamil Nadu
  • Dedicated placement assistance until you get placed
  • Live project experience during every course
