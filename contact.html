Common Questions

Quick answers to what most students ask before enrolling.

What is the course duration?

Most courses run for 3 to 6 months depending on the programme. Flexible weekend batches are also available.

Yes. We provide 100% placement support including resume preparation, mock interviews, and direct company tie-ups.

Is there an EMI option for fees?

We offer easy instalment plans for all courses. Contact our team to find an option that suits your budget.

What certificate will I receive?

You receive an industry-recognised certificate from NewGen Wings Technology upon successful course completion.

Can working professionals join?

Absolutely. We run weekend and evening batches designed specifically for working professionals.

Do I need prior coding experience?

No prior experience is needed for most courses. We start from the basics and build up at a comfortable pace.
