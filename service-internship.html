PROGRAM OVERVIEW

Work on Real Projects, Not Just Theory

Our internship program bridges the gap between classroom learning and industry expectations. You'll be embedded in real project workflows — writing code, attending stand-ups, and solving actual business problems under the guidance of senior engineers.

Every intern receives a verified experience letter on completion, which is recognised by our 50+ corporate hiring partners when you apply for jobs.

Live Project Work

Work on real client or product projects — not dummy exercises. Your contributions matter.

Mentor-Led Guidance

A senior engineer mentors you throughout, reviewing your code and helping you improve daily.

Verified Certificate

Receive an industry-recognised experience letter that boosts your resume immediately.

Flexible Duration

Choose 1, 3, or 6-month tracks to match your academic schedule or availability.

WHAT'S INCLUDED

Everything You Get

Live Project Assignment

You are assigned to an active project from day one. Work in a real team environment with version control, code reviews, and sprint planning.

Dedicated Industry Mentor

A professional with 5+ years experience guides you — answering technical questions, reviewing your work, and helping you build professional habits.

Experience Letter & Certificate

On successful completion you receive a verifiable experience certificate that strengthens your job applications and LinkedIn profile.

GitHub Portfolio Building

All your internship work is committed to your personal GitHub. Leave with a live portfolio of real contributions to show employers.

Soft Skills Coaching

Communication, email etiquette, presentation skills, and professional conduct — covered alongside technical work.

Placement Referral

Top-performing interns are directly referred to our hiring partner companies at the end of the program.

HOW IT WORKS

Your Internship Journey

1

Apply & Orientation

Submit your application online or in person. We assess your current skills and assign you to the most appropriate project track.

2

Onboarding & Setup

Set up your development environment, meet your mentor and team, and get a full briefing on the project scope and your role.

3

Active Project Work

Contribute to sprints, attend daily standups, complete assigned tasks, and receive weekly code reviews from your mentor.

4

Mid-Review

A formal review at the halfway point evaluates your progress, identifies areas for improvement, and adjusts your tasks accordingly.

5

Project Presentation

Present your completed work to the team. Build confidence in communicating technical outcomes to a professional audience.

6

Certificate & Placement

Receive your verified experience certificate. Top performers are fast-tracked to our placement support program.
